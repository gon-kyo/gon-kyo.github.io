About The Site
This site emerged from the mind of the user Gonkyo. Within the boredom and isolation of the global pandemic of 2020-2021, the user decided they would do something productive and leave their imprint on the digital world. Here you can find a catalog of different writings, scraps, poetry, prose, thoughts, or whatever innane nonsense Gonkyo has decided to put on these pages. If you wish for a deep dive into the mind of a person who has lived as a shutin for about a year, look no further. The developer is also has a proclivity for codes, so if you are interested, considder taking a look at the Secret of the day page, for a hidden message that takes a little more work to read.
